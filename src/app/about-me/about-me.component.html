About Me

My several years of experience as a CNC turner and the process-oriented view of workflows that come with it contributed to my decision to pursue an education in IT. As an avid car/tuning enthusiast, my interest in software solutions and new technical innovations is very high and a constant companion in my life. During my IT training, I quickly realized that analytical thinking and recognizing logical connections are my strengths, and these are the challenges I would like to face in the future. Within the framework of various project-based learning methods, I was able to implement various software projects both in a team and independently.
